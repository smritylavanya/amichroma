EDM Night with DJ Jorge Leon

Hold on to your breaths! Because the widely acclaimed and tenacious Jorge Leon is about to grace us all at AMICHROMA 2019. The Spanish DJ and Producer has dedicated several years of his life to creating and revamping music. He has experience playing in festivals all around the world such as Oceanbeat and Arena Sound and collaborating with mainstream artists such as Martin Garrix, Steve Aoki, Bad Bunny, The Vamps among countless others. And with his impressive discography adorned with the latest single 'It's Lit', he promises for an evening nothing short of extraordinary. Come be a part of this emmersive experience at AMICHROMA 2019.

Highlights of Chroma 2k18

Chroma is Amity University's biggest annual Cultural Festival and has established itself as a legacy where everyone looks forward to coming together be it, competing or enjoying. This Extravagant Fest offered a rich blend of music, art and dance including 55+ events, amazing stage shows 2 EDM nights and a special KTM stunt show. Chroma promises an exuberant garland of  learning and never-ending entertainment.Last year the fest also had 35+ sponsors and prizes worth Rs.2 lakh. Making Amichroma 2018 one of the grandiose cultural affair in the city. Continuing with the tradition of providing an academically and culturally stimulating atmosphere, we hope to build on the grand success of the previous years's Chroma and make this one, more magnificent.

#IAmwithher
This year Chroma has decided to stand for Women Empowerment, that makes the need for change imperative.

- Awareness
The Team mates of Chroma have pledged to be the face of the youth and incessantly bring awareness about issues of societal imbalance. We want things to get back on track.

- Charity
Chroma 2K19 has a soft corner too. The need of the hour is to make sure that nobody is left behind. While the fun and laughter goes on, the society must equally join in. Prioritizing this idea, the organisers have planned to donate 2-5% of the total collection to charity that shall would be distributed amongst NGOs.

- ZeroTolerance
We’re strictly against nuisance, harassment and intimidation of any sort and if something doesn’t bode well then anyone’s free to contact the organizing committee at any point as a sincere clause of “Zer0-Tolerance” would be followed, throughout the event.
