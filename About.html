AMICHROMA

AmiChroma is a 2 day youth fest,organized by Amity University Madhya Pradesh,celebrating the energy, vigour and excitement of youth in all it's glory. With over 50interactive events under the belt, EDM nights, MUN, Nukkad natak to name a few, Amichroma is a brimming experience that lets you bring out the best version of yourself. All roads lead to AUMP when amichroma is around.

Thousand of students from various Schools, Colleges,Universities, including the other Amity campuses, participate in this enthralling carnival that celebrates life. With performances from Mainstream Contemporary artists to amateur bands,every moment at amichroma is unforgettable And Insta-worthy.

Prizes worth 2 Lacs make it an encouraging platform for budding artists and young minds to showcase their talents and give them a push to strive for stars.

A Fest. A Carnival. An Experience.
