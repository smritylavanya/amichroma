SOCIAL INITIATIVES

#I’mwithher
This year Chroma has decided to stand for Women Empowerment, that makes the need for change imperative.

- Awareness
The Team mates of Chroma have pledged to be the face of the youth and incessantly bring awareness about issues of societal imbalance. We want things to get back on track.

- Charity
Chroma 2K19 has a soft corner too. The need of the hour is to make sure that nobody is left behind. While the fun and laughter goes on, the society must equally join in. Prioritizing this idea, the organisers have planned to donate 2-5% of the total collection to charity that shall would be distributed amongst NGOs.

- ZeroTolerance
We’re strictly against nuisance, harassment and intimidation of any sort and if something doesn’t bode well then anyone’s free to contact the organizing committee at any point as a sincere clause of “Zer0-Tolerance” would be followed, throughout the event.

Nostalgia

Have you ever wanted to travel back in time and rewind the clock to your best hours? Because Amity Madhya Pradesh is all set to quench your thirst with its ultimate 2-day fest brimming with vintage vibes. From Polka-dots, funky hair-dos to Chacha Chouwdhary and Pikachu, we have it all. With over 40 events, Chroma: Nostalgia would help you retrospect the chills out of you.

So who said you need a time-machine? Come join us in this fantabulous fiesta and spend these two days reminiscing about Mickey Mouse, black and white TV, Candy Floss and the times you spent playing outdoors.

For this year’s gala, get ready to be transported back in time. Introducing the brand-new, re- imagined Chroma: Nostalgia!
